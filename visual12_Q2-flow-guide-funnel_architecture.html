Key insight: Every Tier 1 topic follows the same structural pattern: a TOFU anchor establishes the category framing, MOFU articles build education and evaluation criteria, and BOFU articles provide specific comparison and decision-support content. Bridge articles (purple cards) connect the blog to solution-oriented site sections, creating pathways from education to evaluation. The CMMS column is deliberately BOFU-heavy because the largest content gap identified in the analysis was in evaluation-stage CMMS content. The Condition Monitoring column carries the most MOFU depth because Q1 established the model and Q2 extends it with sensor-specific and technique-specific educational content. Glossary pages (left) feed definitions into TOFU anchors, while bridge articles (right) route readers toward /en/solutions, /en/competitors, and /en/maintenance-calculators.
