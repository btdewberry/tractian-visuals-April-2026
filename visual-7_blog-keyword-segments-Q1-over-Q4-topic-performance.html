Blog Keyword Segments: Q1 2026 vs Q4 2025 Performance by Topical Area

Click change and impression change across keyword-segmented blog URL groups.

Sorted by click change descending. Blue bars (left axis) show click change. Orange bars (right axis) show impression change. Segments with dark outlines are strategically highlighted.

Key insight: The comparison content segment (best, top, alt, vs) is the single healthiest format: 63 of 75 URLs gained clicks. The monitoring/sensor segment showed the most uniform growth: all 66 URLs gained impressions, and 53 of 66 gained clicks with only 2 losing. The "types" segment is the only group with negative click change despite strong impression growth (+327,286), making it the clearest segment-level capture gap. Q-words and maintenance lead in absolute click gains due to their larger URL counts.
