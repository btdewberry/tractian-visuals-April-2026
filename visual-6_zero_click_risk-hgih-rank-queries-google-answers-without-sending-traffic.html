Zero-Click Risk: High-Ranking Queries Where Google Answers Without Sending Traffic

Queries where Tractian ranks in the top 3 positions with 1,000 or more impressions but receives a click-through rate of 0.1% or lower. Bars show Q1 2026 impression volume. Position, CTR, absolute clicks, and click change are displayed alongside each bar.

Key insight: These queries rank in the top 3 but produce effectively zero clicks. This pattern indicates AI Overview or featured snippet displacement, where Google surfaces the answer directly from the page content. The lead example, "what is a root cause analysis," generated 343,348 impressions at position 1.4 and produced exactly 1 click. This is why the Q2 plan shifts top-of-funnel content toward the glossary and specific applications rather than broad definitional head terms. Pure "what is" content at the top of the funnel is increasingly a zero-click category, and the blog's production emphasis belongs in mid-funnel and bottom-of-funnel formats where click-through rates remain healthy.
