Key insight: Every Tier 1 topic has coverage at multiple funnel stages, ensuring no topic is served by a single content type at a single stage of the reader's journey. MOFU is the largest segment across all tiers, reflecting the Q2 plan's emphasis on education and criteria-setting content. BOFU articles are concentrated in Tier 1 and Tier 3, where comparison and evaluation content addresses the largest identified content gaps (particularly in CMMS and condition monitoring). The MOFU/BOFU bridge articles in each tier connect educational blog content to solution-oriented site sections.
