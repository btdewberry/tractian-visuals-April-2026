Capture Gap Matrix: Where Impressions Are Growing but Clicks Are Not

Top 50 blog pages by Q1 2026 impression volume. Each dot is one page plotted by its Q1-over-Q4 impression change (x-axis) and click change (y-axis). Pages below the zero line with high x-values represent growing search demand that the current content is failing to convert.

Key insight: Pages in the lower-right zone have the most urgent refresh need. Google is showing them to more people, but fewer are clicking.
