Query Cluster Landscape: Where Search Demand Is Growing and Where the Blog Is Capturing It

Each bubble represents a topical query cluster. Position shows Q1-over-Q4 change in aggregate clicks (x-axis) and impressions (y-axis). Bubble size represents total 12-month impression volume, not just the change. The upper-right quadrant is the ideal zone (both growing). The CMMS bubble (red outline) highlights the largest capture gap.

Key insight: Root Cause and Quality Analysis dominates impression growth (+379,926) but its click capture (+96) is disproportionately small, largely due to AI Overview displacement on head terms. CMMS and Software shows moderate impression growth with minimal click conversion, confirming the content gap identified in both the pages and queries analysis. Clusters in the upper-right with balanced growth (Asset Management, SOP, Glossary-Aligned) represent the healthiest expansion candidates.
